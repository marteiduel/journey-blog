Hungry Budget Admin Panel

Technologies used:

ReactJs, Javascript, Redux, Git, Github, PHP, Media Queries, Custom API, HTML, CSS, UI/UX, SQL, Postman

Responsibilities:

Hungry Budget Admin Panel is a full-stack solo project built using ReactJS for the frontend and PHP for the backend. It utilizes Redux or Context for state management and ensures a smooth user interface (UI) and user experience (UX). The application's database is powered by myphpadmin and hosted on Hostigner, enabling efficient data management using SQL. User authentication is implemented to ensure secure access, and HTML and CSS contribute to the application's functionality and design.

The admin panel provides real-time data display for restaurants, allowing them to monitor the performance of their dishes on the Hungry Budget app. Through analytics and statistics, restaurant owners can gain insights into dish popularity and make informed decisions about their menu offerings.

Restaurant owners can easily manage their business's basic information, such as name, address, and logo, through the admin panel. This functionality ensures that the restaurant's details are always up to date and accurately represented on the Hungry Budget platform.

The admin panel grants granular control over the dishes displayed on Hungry Budget. Restaurant owners can modify various aspects of each dish, including name, description, price, and picture. This flexibility allows them to quickly respond to customer feedback, adapt to changing trends, and effectively market their dishes.

The admin panel provides a dedicated subdomain for each restaurant, facilitating seamless integration between their website and the Hungry Budget platform. Additionally, restaurant owners can easily upload new dishes and remove existing ones, ensuring that their menu remains up to date on the Hungry Budget app.

Master Stylist's

Technologies used:

Wordpres, SEO, Analytics, Hostinger, Media Queries, HTML, CSS, UI/UX, Marketing

Responsibilities:

When I began my collaboration with Master Stylist, a local hair salon, from February 9, 2022, to January 24, 2023, the business was primarily brick-and-mortar with minimal online presence. Over this period, I built a robust digital platform for them that attracted 1,258 users, more than half of which came from organic search. This new online presence, combined with data-driven marketing strategies, resulted in a 20% increase in sales, highlighting the impact of a well-planned digital transformation.

The owner of the salon was not tech-inclined, which presented a unique challenge. It was essential to communicate the potential benefits of a digital presence in a way she could understand. Over time, through patience and clear explanations, she began to see the value of the digital world for her business.

Creating the salon's online identity involved building a website on WordPress, hosted by Hostinger. I designed a custom WordPress theme that was ADA compliant and resonated with the salon's brand image. A considerable effort was put into the website's SEO to increase its visibility and attract potential customers.

The website was more than just an online business card; it was a source of valuable data. With the help of Google Analytics, I gathered insights about user behavior and used this information to develop targeted marketing strategies. The shift from a general marketing approach to a data-driven one proved to be highly effective, maximizing the return on marketing investment.

By the end of our collaboration, the salon's owner had not only seen the benefits of digital transformation but also felt empowered to manage her own digital presence. While I continue to be a trusted advisor, it's inspiring to see her confidently navigate her digital journey. This project stands as a testament to my ability to drive digital transformation, communicate effectively with diverse audiences, and deliver tangible results.
